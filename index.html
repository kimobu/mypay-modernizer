MyPay Modernizer

MyPay for the modern web

MyPay Modernizer takes the Defense Finance and Accounting Service's myPay website and redesigns it for the modern web. The things you want to do and the information you want to see are now displayed prominently on the pages.

Responsive

MyPay Modernizer makes the myPay website responsive. The original website wastes screen real estate with whitespace and fixed-size elements. The modernized site scales content to your device's viewport.

Freedom level: over 9000

MyPay Modernizer returns your freedom by:

  • giving you back the power to right-click.
  • opening in a new tab instead of a new window
  • not opening in full screen for no reason

Simplified LES

MyPay Modernizer reformats the LES so you can actually read the information. No longer is your pay information stored in a table within a table within a table. No longer are the font sizes so small that you need BCGs to read them.
